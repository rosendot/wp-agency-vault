# Client Contracts

## Where contracts live

Contract templates are stored in Google Drive:

```
Google Drive/
└── Agency/
    ├── Contracts/
    │   ├── _templates/
    │   │   └── Service Agreement Template.docx
    │   └── Clients/
    │       └── [Client Name] - Signed Agreement.pdf
    ├── Proposals/
    └── Invoices/
```

## Signing

Use Google Workspace eSignature (built into Google Docs) to send contracts for signature. Check your Workspace plan — it may already be included. If not, use Dropbox Sign (~$15/mo).

## What the contract must cover

- Scope of work (what is and isn't included)
- Monthly retainer amount ($399/mo) and billing date
- What happens if client stops paying (site goes offline after X days)
- IP ownership — custom theme stays agency property, client owns their domain/email/GA4
- Cancellation terms — how much notice is required
- Liability limits — agency is not liable for POS outages, client data loss, etc.
- Content responsibility — client is responsible for accuracy of their own content

## Before every new client

1. Duplicate the template in Google Drive
2. Fill in client name, business name, site URL, start date
3. Send via eSignature
4. File the signed PDF in `Contracts/Clients/`
5. Do not begin work until signed contract is received
