Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Q

What are your hours?

Mon–Fri 11am–10pm, Sat–Sun 10am–11pm. Closed major holidays.

Q

Do you take reservations?

Yes, call us or book online. Walk-ins welcome. Reservations recommended for 6+.

Q

Do you offer catering?

We cater events of all sizes. Contact us for custom menus and pricing.

Q

Is there parking?

Free lot behind the building (30 spaces). Street parking also available.

Q

Dietary restrictions?

Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available. Tell your server about allergies.

Q

Private events?

Private dining room seats 24 with dedicated server and AV equipment.
